Lost Souls Found The Gold You Lost To Save Your Soul

Level 1

Foolish self! After years of gathering coins, your soul has been tainted with money.
To redeem your peace of mind, you must lay down all of your coins.
The greedy Pac-Men will happily collect them, but don't hit them or you'll get their count back!

Controls: arrow keys to move, P to pause/unpause the game
